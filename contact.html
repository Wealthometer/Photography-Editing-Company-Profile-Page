FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common questions about my services

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and major events, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For portrait sessions and smaller projects, 2-4 weeks notice is usually sufficient. However, I sometimes have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to reach out even if your event is coming up soon.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include professional photography, basic editing, and an online gallery for viewing and downloading. Specific inclusions vary by package type - wedding packages include engagement sessions, while portrait packages include wardrobe consultation. Detailed package information is available on my services page.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! I love traveling for photography projects. I cover the entire Los Angeles area at no extra charge. For destinations outside LA, travel fees may apply depending on the location and duration. I'm always excited to explore new places and capture beautiful moments wherever they may be.

How long until I receive my photos?

Turnaround times vary by project type. Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 1-2 weeks, while wedding galleries are ready in 4-6 weeks. For commercial projects, delivery time depends on the scope and editing requirements. I always provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

Can I request specific editing styles?

While I have my signature editing style, I'm happy to adapt to your preferences. During our consultation, we can discuss your vision and look at examples of styles you like. I want to ensure the final images perfectly match your expectations and aesthetic preferences.

What happens if weather affects our outdoor shoot?

I always monitor weather conditions closely and will communicate with you about any concerns. For light rain, we can often work with umbrellas or find covered areas. For severe weather, we'll reschedule at no additional cost. I also have backup indoor location options and studio space available when needed.
